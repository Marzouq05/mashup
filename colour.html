Fossil Neutra Automatic

MRP ₹ 20,995.00

The Fossil Neutra Automatic is a stylish mechanical watch that combines classic design with modern craftsmanship. It features an automatic movement, which means it is powered by the motion of the wearer's wrist rather than a battery. The watch often includes a skeleton dial or exhibition case back, allowing users to see the intricate movement inside. Its durable construction and elegant appearance make it suitable for both casual and formal occasions. Overall, the Fossil Neutra Automatic is a popular choice for watch enthusiasts who appreciate traditional watchmaking and timeless aesthetics.
