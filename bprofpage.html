Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior, known as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Santos and the Brazil national team. Celebrated for his dribbling, creativity, and finishing, he starred for Santos, Barcelona’s treble-winning “MSN” attack, and PSG after a world-record €222m transfer. Brazil’s all-time top scorer with 79 goals, he won Olympic gold in 2016 and major titles in Brazil, Spain, and France.
